Pulchra
=======

Physical, Stun

Summary:

-   +30% DMG
-   +30% CRIT DMG (King of the Summit 4PC)
-   While Pulchra has some supportive utility, she really just deals damage and Daze. Most of her mindscapes improve her personal damage.
-   PITA to play
-   Evasive Assist makes bosses tougher
-   Aftershock
-   Quick Assists

Core Passive:

-   Hunter's Gait from EX Special, Assist Follow-up (1 Assist Point), Chain Attack, Ultimate
-   Quick Assist from heavy attack in Hunter's Gait

Additional Ability (Attack, Rupture):

-   +30% Aftershock DMG

Assist:

-   Assist Follow-up: Use 1 Assist Point to enter Hunter's Gait

Special Attack:

-   Series of aftershock hits when another active character hits with (EX) Special

Mindscapes:

-   M6: Additional Ability applies to all damage

Weapons:

-   Blazing Laurel: +18% Impact, +25% Impact, on Quick/Perfect Assist, 713 ATK
-   Box Cutter: +24% Physical DMG, +16% Daze, 624 ATK

Drive Disks:

-   4PC King of the Summit
-   2PC Shockstar Disco
-   Another supportive Disk Drive
