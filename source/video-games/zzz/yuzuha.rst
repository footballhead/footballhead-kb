Yuzuha
======

Physical, Support (Anomaly)

Summary:

-   +1200 ATK
-   +15% DMG
-   +17.2% Anomaly Buildup Rate (F2P)
-   +17.2% Anomaly DMG (F2P)
-   +17.2% Disorder DMG (F2P)
-   Aftershock every 8s
-   Increased Anomaly application via Sugarburst Sparkles (ticks every 1s, has +25% buildup)
-   Coordinated attacks from Tanuki
-   Time-based Assist Followup for a lot more damage
-   Choice of slow or long EX Special Attack based on playstyle.
-   Ultimate gives energy to squad

.. note:: Need 3000 ATK for full Core Passive effect. Since Yuzuha is an anomaly support, the more the better!

.. note:: Need 200 AM for full Additional Ability effect. The best you can do F2P is 171.

    -   124 at Level 60, Core F
    -   +8% from Phaethon Melody 2PC
    -   +30% from slot 6
    -   30 from signature W-Engine S1

    Without her W-Engine, you can only hit 171 AM. With her signture W-Engine at P1, this configuration hits 212 AM.

Core Skill Core Passive:

-   1200 ATK for 40s from EX Special/Ultimate
-   +15% DMG for 40s from EX Special/Ultimate

Core Skill Additional Ability (Anomaly, Spook Shack):

-   +20% Anomaly Buildup Rate (caps at 200 AM)
-   +20% Anomaly DMG (caps at 200 AM)
-   +20% Disorder DMG (caps at 200 AM)

.. note:: As F2P, since you can only hit 171 AM, these are capped at 17.2%
